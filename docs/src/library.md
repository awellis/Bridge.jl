## Online statistics

Online updating of the tuple `state = (m, m2, n)` where

`m` - `mean(x[1:n])`

`m2` - sum of squares of differences from the current mean, ``\textstyle\sum_{i=1}^n (x_i - \bar x_n)^2``

`n` - number of iterations
